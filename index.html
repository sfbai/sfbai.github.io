About

Safety First bAI develops learning systems that come with measurable safety guarantees. We focus on real-world robotics where uncertainty, edge-cases, and stakes are high. Our approach blends formal verification, constrained learning, and robust simulation-to-reality transfer.

Technology

Our stack pairs provable control primitives with modern representation learning. We build modular toolchains for safe adaptation, online monitoring, and recoverable autonomy—designed so partners can integrate with existing fleets and processes.
